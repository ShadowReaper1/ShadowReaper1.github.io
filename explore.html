Developing and Programming

A passion of mine

Ever since i was a kid i was fascinated by the softwares and their working method and wondered how people were able to do amazing things by using workarounds and little hacks to do things that were not meant to be able to run on some hardware like about 10-12 years ago after the release of DirectX 9 release they introduced Pixel Shader 2.0 and at that time my pc was 5 years older than that, no way it had the ability to run something that advanced but then one day i found a dll online that lets you play Pixel Shader 2.0 games so i decided to try it on PCSX2(A playstation 2 emulator for pc.) well guess what? it worked and i was amazed. Although my pc could'nt handle it all and ran games at like 2-9fps and still being the way i am i played Dragon Ball Infinite World and other games. Well that is one of the many stories that lead me to this path. I am inactive from mid 2018 and been that way ever since because of some personal reasons but before that i used to develop roms and kernel for my devices. I will return in 2020 and start doing various stuffs again and make up for the time i've lost. I already have various ideas for projects and interesting things ;)
